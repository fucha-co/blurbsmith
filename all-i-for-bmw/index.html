ALL i, for BMW





“ALL i” is a BLURBSMITH concept ad for the BMW i SERIES.

While my Christmas wish didn’t come true this year, I still love this simple 3 part campaign. I’m sure Santa came good on this for more than a few others who wished for a new i Series BMW.

I’ve proudly lived car-free for 15 years and I feel much greener for it. I’ve been contemplating ownership again recently and my two critical criteria are fuel economy and environmental footprint. So when I learned of the BWM i Series, I fell in love: a car that uses no fuel — for up to 360km — is very green and very me. And while the i8 is beyond reach for many, the i3 is within reach for most… especially given the long-term fuel and environmental savings.

You can read more about the i3 range on the official BMW website: www.bmw.com.au/i
